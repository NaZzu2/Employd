# **App Name**: ShiftFinder

## Core Features:

- Employee Profile & CV Management: Employees can create, edit, and view their professional profiles, including work experience, skills, and upload CV documents.
- Job Discovery & Catalog: Employees can browse a curated feed of available jobs from employers, with filtering options and detailed views of job postings.
- Employer Pinging System: Employees can express interest in a job by sending a 'ping' notification to the employer without direct contact, indicating availability or interest.
- Secure User Authentication: Enable secure sign-up and login for employees using standard methods (e.g., email/password) to protect their data and profiles.
- AI-Powered Job Recommendations: An AI tool that suggests relevant job postings to employees based on their profile data, skills, and past interactions to improve matching.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Slate Blue (#262E38) to convey professionalism and reliability, providing a strong anchor for the interface.
- Background color: Light Grayish Blue (#F2F3F4) offering a clean and modern backdrop that enhances readability and content focus.
- Accent color: Vibrant Sky Blue (#37C3E6) to highlight interactive elements, calls-to-action, and signify positive user actions like a 'ping'.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and highly readable design, ensuring information is conveyed efficiently.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist line icons to represent job categories, profile sections, and interactions, aiding quick visual comprehension.
- Mobile-first, card-based layout for job listings and profile details, designed for intuitive navigation and optimal legibility on small screens. Implement swipe gestures for job browsing where appropriate.
- Subtle and functional micro-interactions, such as a smooth transition when 'pinging' an employer or gentle fade-ins for content loading, to enhance the app's responsiveness and perceived fluidity.
